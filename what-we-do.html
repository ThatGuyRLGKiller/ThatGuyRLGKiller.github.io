About Us

We are a gaming community with teams for various games. Here's what we do and what we stand for:

Our Activities

  • We have a Guild in Guild Wars 2 for players to team up and explore.
  • We run a Minecraft Modpack and a server for our members to enjoy—at least for now!
  • We host gaming events as often as we can to keep the community engaged and connected.
  • We proudly manage a Discord Guild where members can hang out, chat, and share their love for gaming.
  • We're into anime and coding, adding a little extra to our gaming experience!

Our Values

Red Light Gaming is a community for people who prefer to chill and play instead of sweating their asses off trying to win. If you're someone who enjoys a competitive gaming environment, you're welcome here! However, we do have one rule: don't be a dick. Treat everyone with respect and enjoy the vibe.
